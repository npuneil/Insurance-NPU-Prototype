EXECUTIVE Q&A – ANTICIPATED QUESTIONS & ANSWERS
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  Q: "How accurate is this compared to cloud AI?"
  A: "The on-device model handles routine triage — severity, cost
     estimates, next steps. Think of it as first-pass AI for the 80%
     of standard claims. Complex or ambiguous cases still escalate to
     a senior adjuster or cloud model. The value is instant triage at
     zero cost."

  Q: "What about model updates?"
  A: "Models are deployed through standard device management. When a
     new model ships, it's pushed to the fleet like any software
     update. No re-training infrastructure needed."

  Q: "Does this replace cloud AI?"
  A: "No — it complements it. Routine, high-volume tasks run on the
     NPU at zero marginal cost. Complex tasks or batch processing
     still go to the cloud. You're offloading the most repetitive
     work from your most expensive infrastructure."

  Q: "What hardware is this running on?"
  A: "This is a Microsoft Surface Copilot+ PC with a Qualcomm
     Snapdragon NPU. It's already in our commercial lineup — this
     is shipping hardware, not a prototype chip."

  Q: "What about data privacy / compliance?"
  A: "All inference is local. Customer PII, claim photos, policy
     documents — nothing leaves the device. No cross-border data
     transfer, no cloud storage of sensitive data. This simplifies
     compliance significantly."

  Q: "What's the per-device cost?"
  A: "The NPU is built into every Copilot+ PC — there's no separate
     license or hardware add-on. If Zava is already purchasing
     Surface devices, the AI capability is included."

  Q: "What about the carbon / sustainability angle?"
  A: "The NPU draws about 3 watts versus 300+ watts for a cloud GPU.
     At fleet scale, the dashboard shows the CO₂ reduction — it's
     the equivalent of thousands of fewer miles driven per year."
